Child Safety Standards

Last updated: 2026-02-27

CSAE/CSAM Zero tolerance Reporting

1) Zero tolerance policy

GooZoo has a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior involving child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), including child sexual abuse material (CSAM). We prohibit:

  • Any sexual content involving minors, including imagery, messages, or attempts to solicit such content
  • Grooming, coercion, or exploitation of minors in any form
  • Sharing, requesting, or distributing CSAM or links to CSAM
  • Any facilitation of CSAE, including instructions or encouragement

2) Reporting child safety concerns

If you believe you have encountered CSAE/CSAM or any child safety concern related to GooZoo, report it immediately:

  • Email: mehran@mehrandroid.com
  • Subject line: GooZoo Child Safety Report
  • Please include any relevant details (screenshots, membership codes, timestamps, and a brief description).

We review reports as quickly as possible and may request additional context if needed.

3) Enforcement

When we become aware of content or behavior that violates these standards, we may take actions including:

  • Removing content where applicable
  • Restricting or terminating access for offending users
  • Preserving relevant information for legal compliance
  • Reporting to appropriate regional or national authorities when required by applicable law

4) Age guidance

GooZoo is intended for adults (18+). We do not aim to target children as the primary audience.
